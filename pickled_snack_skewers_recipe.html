Pickled Snack Skewers

Party Perfect

üìù Ingredients

  • 200g pickled cucamelons
  • 200g cheese cubes (cheddar or gouda)
  • 100g pitted olives (green or black)
  • Wooden skewers or toothpicks
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (optional)

üç¥ Preparation

  1. Prepare ingredients: Drain pickled cucamelons and pat dry. Cut cheese into bite-sized cubes.
  2. Thread onto skewers: Alternate threading pickled cucamelons, cheese cubes, and olives onto wooden skewers or toothpicks.
  3. Arrange on platter: Place finished skewers on a serving platter in a decorative pattern.
  4. Garnish: Add fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme sprigs for extra visual appeal.
  5. Serve chilled: Keep refrigerated until ready to serve. Perfect for parties and gatherings!
