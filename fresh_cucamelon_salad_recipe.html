Fresh Cucamelon Salad

Fresh & Delicious üåø

üìù Ingredients

  • 100ml yogurt
  • 100ml mayo
  • 50ml fresh cream
  • Fresh dill
  • Red onions
  • Purple olives
  • 250g fresh cucamelon
  • 150g pickled cucamelon
  • 30ml salad dressing

üç¥ Preparation

  1. Prepare the dressing: In a bowl, mix yogurt, mayo, and fresh cream until smooth and creamy.
  2. Prep the ingredients: Chop fresh dill finely, slice red onions thinly, and halve the purple olives.
  3. Combine cucamelons: In a large salad bowl, add the fresh cucamelons (whole or halved) and pickled cucamelons.
  4. Mix it all together: Add the chopped dill, red onions, and olives to the cucamelons.
  5. Add the dressing: Pour the creamy dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  6. Final touch: Drizzle with salad dressing and toss once more.
  7. Chill & serve: Refrigerate for 15-20 minutes before serving for best flavor. Garnish with extra dill!
