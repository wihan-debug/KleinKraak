Cucamelon Taco Topping

Tangy Twist

üìù Ingredients

  • 200g pickled cucamelons, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, finely diced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Pinch of chili flakes (optional)
  • Your favorite taco fillings

üç¥ Preparation

  1. Slice the cucamelons: Cut pickled cucamelons into thin slices or quarters depending on preference.
  2. Dice the onion: Finely chop red onion and mix with sliced cucamelons in a bowl.
  3. Add flavor: Toss with fresh cilantro, lime juice, and optional chili flakes for extra kick.
  4. Let it marinate: Allow the mixture to sit for 5-10 minutes to let flavors meld.
  5. Assemble tacos: Prepare your favorite tacos (fish, chicken, beef, or veggie) and top generously with the cucamelon mixture.
  6. Serve & enjoy: The tangy, crunchy cucamelons add the perfect zing to any taco!
