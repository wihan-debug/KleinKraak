Cucamelon Cocktail Garnish

Sophisticated

üìù Ingredients

  • Fresh or pickled cucamelons
  • Cocktail picks or skewers
  • Your favorite gin or vodka cocktail
  • Fresh herbs (mint, basil, or rosemary)
  • Citrus slices (optional)

üç¥ Preparation

  1. Prepare cucamelons: Rinse fresh cucamelons or drain pickled ones. Pat dry with paper towel.
  2. Thread on picks: Skewer 2-3 cucamelons onto cocktail picks. You can alternate with herbs or citrus for variety.
  3. Prepare your cocktail: Make your favorite gin martini, vodka tonic, or any spirit-forward cocktail.
  4. Garnish creatively: Rest the cucamelon pick across the rim of the glass or place it directly in the drink.
  5. Serve with style: The unique garnish adds visual appeal and a conversation starter!

üí° Pro Tips

  • Fresh cucamelons: Best for gin cocktails - adds cucumber-lime notes
  • Pickled cucamelons: Perfect for dirty martinis or savory drinks - adds tangy flavor
  • Muddle option: Muddle 2-3 cucamelons in the shaker for extra flavor infusion
