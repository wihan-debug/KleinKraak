Growing Fresh at KleinKraak

Elandsfontein, South Africa

From Seed to Snack

KleinKraak started with a simple idea: bring the unique, refreshing taste of cucamelons to South Africa. These tiny grape-sized melons, also known as "mouse melons," have been a family favorite for generations in Mexico.

Based in Elandsfontein, we grow our cucamelons with care, harvesting them at peak freshness. We then transform some into our signature pickled varieties using traditional brining methods and quality ingredients.

Every jar and every fresh batch represents our commitment to quality, sustainability, and bringing something truly special to your table.
